CAPITAL RAISING

VCG offers a comprehensive capital raising service to help MSMEs access the financing they need to grow and succeed. We work closely with our clients to understand their unique funding requirements and to develop tailored solutions that meet their specific needs. Our team has extensive experience in securing capital from a variety of sources, including banks, microfinance institutions, and grants.

Financial Analysis:

We conduct a thorough financial analysis of our clients' businesses to understand their funding needs and to identify the most appropriate sources of capital.

Funding Strategy:

Funding Applications:

We assist our clients with preparing and submitting funding applications to banks, microfinance institutions, and grant programs, ensuring that all required documentation is provided and that applications are submitted on time.

Monitoring and Reporting:

We monitor our clients' financing arrangements and provide regular
